Pricing Plans

Experience the thrill of a lifetime with our incredible rafting service, where adventure meets exhilaration! Our rafting excursions offer an unbeatable blend of excitement, natural beauty, and expert guidance that will leave you craving for more. Get ready to conquer roaring rapids, navigate through breathtaking canyons, and create unforgettable memories with friends and family.

Price Plan 1

At our rafting service, we understand that every adventurer has unique preferences, which is why we offer three enticing price plans to suit your needs. The "Explorer" plan is perfect for those seeking an introductory rafting experience. Priced at just $49 per person, this plan covers a thrilling half-day adventure, ensuring you get a taste of the adrenaline rush while exploring the picturesque river landscapes.

Price Plan 2

For the more adventurous souls, the "Adventurer" plan takes the experience up a notch. Priced at $99 per person, this full-day package includes a heart-pounding journey through challenging rapids, complemented by serene stretches where you can soak in the beauty of nature. Our experienced guides will be by your side, ensuring your safety while maximizing the excitement.

Price Plan 3

If you're seeking the ultimate rafting escapade, our "Extreme" plan is tailor-made for you. Priced at $149 per person, this premium package offers an action-packed two-day adventure, complete with camping under the stars, gourmet meals, and an extended rafting expedition. Brace yourself for an adrenaline-fueled experience like no other, where you'll conquer powerful rapids and immerse yourself in the untamed wilderness.
